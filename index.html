    TRIP SCHEDULE


    July 2017, Family Trip - Norway

    Route Trip Locations

    • Oslo
    • Bergen
    • Stavanger
    • Fiords Central Area
    • Back To Oslo

    Day 1, Wednesday 05/07 - Landing in Oslo

    Landed in Norway at night, and arriving to "Park Inn by Radisson Oslo Airport Hotel"

    Day 2, Thursday 06/07 - Traveling with a train to Bergen

    Exploring the old city + going to the fish market. Stayed in Zander K hotel.

    Day 3, Friday 07/07 - Continue the tour in Bergen

    Climbing the mountain overlooking the city, Rent a car and drive to Stavanger. Stayed at Clarion Hotel Stavanger.

    Day 4, Saturday 08/07 - Shabbat

    Walking around the old city, Visiting Museums. Stayed at Clarion Hotel Stavanger.

    Day 5, Sunday 09/07 - Travel & Trek

    Around 5 hours trek at Pulpit Rock (Preikestolen), and traveling to the Fiords area. Stayed at - Hartanger Hostel B&B.

    Day 6, Monday 10/07 - Visiting Eidjord & Skytejefossen

    Visiting Eidjford town, And Skytjefossen waterfall. Also Voringsfossen waterfal, And Kipasen farm.

    Day 7, Tuesday 11/07 - Waterfall trek

    Trek at Hardangervidda, Of 4 different waterfalls, And a scenic drive called "Jondal Drive" between Utne to Jondal. Stayed at Voss Vandrarheim Hostel.

    Day 8, Wednesday 12/07 - Visiting Stalheim, Flam & Undredal towns

    Visiting Stalheim,And Flam towns, Traveling with a ferry to Gudvangen, And then to Undredal town. Stayed at Voss Vandrarheim Hostel.

    Day 9, Thursday 13/07 - Scenic drives

    Scenic drives from Snovegen, to Aurland and Laerdal. Visiting Fjaerland town and Supphellebreen glaciers. Stayed at Hjelle Hotel.

    Day 10, Friday 14/07 - Scenic driving tour

    Scenic driving tour from Gamle Strynefjellsvegen, until Geiranger. Stayed at Geiranger.

    Day 11, Saturday 15/07 - Shabbat

    Relaxing, Walking around Geiranger and getting to know the area.

    Day 12, Sunday 16/07 - Traveling to Valladal

    Traveling to Valladal & Stigfossen waterfall. Stayed at Kvila Turistheim.

    Day 13, Monday 17/07 - Besseggen Trek

    Climbing Besseggen mountain Trek, Stayed at Kvila Turistheim.

    Day 14 - Day 16 (18/07 - 20/07) - Driving to Oslo

    Visiting Drobak town, Driving to Oslo, And spending the last 3 days, touring around Oslo. Stayed at Clarion Collection Bastion Hotel. Boarding the plane back to Israel.
